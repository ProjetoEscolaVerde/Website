Our objective

The Escol@Verde project consists of a sort of "tripod"

Automation

An autonomous ambient made by students for the realization of tests and enhancements that seek development, not only for the Escol@Verde Project, but also the students themselves.

Research

An open enviroment where any student can peform research on their own school, been able to use both the sensors and provided equipiment.

Lessons

Not only to promote interdisciplinary classes, with more possibilities of dynamic classes, as to make teaching in the classroom more illustrative. Using data collected at the school by the sensors to use as examples geography and biology classes.
